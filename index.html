Today’s internet is filled with algorithm-driven feeds that often lead to passive, mindless scrolling. This site seeks to change that by creating a self-generating website that focuses on curiosity and exploration.

By taking content from different URLs, this site helps create connections between different seemingly unrelated elements, encouraging us to think about the content in a new way.

Re: represents re-imagining, re-framing, and re-exploring the internet and its content.
